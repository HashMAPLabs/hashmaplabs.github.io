What's #MAP

Mathematics

Computers were built on the principles of Mathematics. Behind every major Programming concept, lies a Mathematical concept that drives it to fruition. Through this, we aren't trying to make you math geniuses, but plan to teach you just enough to heighten your understanding of Programming.

Algorithms

Algorithms are how we communicate with the computers. They are simply mathematical concepts wrapped up with English mnemonics. Hence, one has to master the art of writing algorithms in order to effectively communicate with computers. This segment is here to do just that!

Programming

HashMAP was created to give everyone a Programmer's inituition. Off-late, Programming has become a prominent skill to possess. Keeping this in mind, we are giving you a course which has a bottom-up teaching mechanism and helps one develop strong foundations in Programming.

Our Philosophy

Programming is a form of skill which each and everyone ought to learn. Programming languages, on the other hand, are just tools which are used to showcase these skills. One should always strive to learn the concepts of programming and develop them as a skill rather than falling prey to its rhetorics. We are here to impart these very skills using the three core fundamentals on which Computers are built: Mathematics, Algorithms and Programming. Once equipped with these skills, anyone can pick up a programming language in a matter of days. So let it be Python, Java or Rust, as long as you have these concepts imbibed in you, learning them would be child's play.
